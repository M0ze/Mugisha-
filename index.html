Technical Outreach in Central Uganda

Mubende Engineering Outreach (MEO) operates at the intersection of technical precision and social responsibility. Our objective is to bridge the infrastructure gap in Central Uganda through rigorous engineering standards and sustainable design methodologies.

From complex hydraulic network designs to structural load-bearing analysis for public facilities, our team ensures that every project meets international engineering benchmarks while remaining locally viable.
